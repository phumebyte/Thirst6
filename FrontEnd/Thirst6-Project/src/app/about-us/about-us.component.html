About Thirst6

Our Mission

At Thirst6, our mission is to tackle water scarcity challenges in South Africa through innovative technology. Our smart water management tool helps municipalities track and analyze water usage, promoting efficient water management and encouraging water conservation among users.

Why Thirst6?

With South Africa facing severe water scarcity, Thirst6 provides a crucial solution for municipalities to monitor water usage effectively. Our platform ensures transparency and accountability, allowing users to hold municipalities responsible and make informed decisions about water conservation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where water resources are managed sustainably, and every municipality is empowered to optimize its water distribution. Thirst6 aims to drive impactful change and contribute to a water-secure future for South Africa.
